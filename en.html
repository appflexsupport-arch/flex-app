Step-by-step Guides

How to add a new expense?
  1. Open the main Dashboard screen.
  2. Tap the large blue "+" button at the bottom.
  3. Enter the amount and select a category.
  4. Optionally, add a note (e.g., "Coffee with Mike").
  5. Tap "Save" in the top right corner.
How to change the main currency?
  1. Go to the Settings tab (gear icon).
  2. Under "Appearance & Region", select Main Currency.
  3. Choose a new currency from the list (e.g., EUR or USD).
  4. The app will automatically convert your existing records based on current rates!
How to reset my PIN code?
For security reasons, if you forget your PIN and do not have FaceID/TouchID enabled, the only option is to reinstall the application. Ensure your data is synced with iCloud before doing so.

Privacy Policy

  • Where is your data? All data regarding your transactions, budgets, and accounts is stored locally on your device (and securely backed up to your private iCloud account).
  • What we DO NOT do: We never send your financial data to our servers, we do not analyze it, and we do not sell it to third parties.
  • Analytics: We only collect anonymous crash reports provided by Apple's systems to help us fix bugs faster.
